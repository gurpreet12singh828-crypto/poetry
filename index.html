About Me

My name is Gurpreet Singh. I am a poet and writer who believes poetry is the voice of the soul. Every poem here is inspired by real emotions, silent struggles, love, loneliness and hope.

My Poetry

Unsaid Words

Some words remain silent, Not because they are weak, But because the heart fears What truth might speak.

Loneliness

I walk among a thousand faces, Yet feel alone inside, Loneliness is not being alone, It is being unheard while you hide.

Hope

Even when nights feel endless, And stars refuse to shine, Hope whispers softly, This pain is not forever, it will pass with time.

Love

Love is not always closeness, Sometimes it is distance and pain, Yet choosing the same soul, Again and again.
